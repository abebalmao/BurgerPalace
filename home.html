OUR BURGERS

Our main goal when creating our burgers, is for them to be both economically available to as many people as possible while still maintaining a high level of quality. This is why we have chosen to import our meat from local farms where the meat only has to travel about an hour before they reach our kitchen.

OUR BEER

More than being a michelin chef, Ragnar Ragnarsson also is an avid beer fan. In our basement we have a distillery where we create our own beer. We specialize in creating both Ipa's and lager.

We are not a normal bar. Because even though we love the classics, we think a little bit differently and have different tastes when it comes to making drinks. The bar and the kitchen work close to each other and we do everything we can ourselves - from the hand-cut ice in the drink to the chips and the dip next to it.
